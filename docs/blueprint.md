# **App Name**: S-Com Hub

## Core Features:

- User and Merchant Authentication: Secure login, registration, and role-based access for both customers and merchants.
- Product Catalog Management: Merchants can add, update, and manage product listings with details like variations, images, and categories.
- Dynamic Shopping Cart & Checkout: Customers can add/remove products, proceed through a multi-step checkout process, supporting guest checkout.
- Vietnam-Specific Payment & Shipping: Integration with key local payment gateways (e.g., VNPAY, MoMo, COD) and major shipping providers (e.g., GHN, GHTK).
- Multi-Vendor Storefronts (SaaS MVP): Merchants can create and customize their storefronts accessible via unique subdomains/domains, managed through a basic layout editor.
- Order Management & Fulfillment (Merchant-facing): Merchants can view, confirm, process, and track orders from their dashboard.
- AI Product Assistant Tool: An AI-powered chatbot tool that uses the store's product catalog and policies as a knowledge base to answer customer queries and recommend products.

## Style Guidelines:

- A dark color scheme provides a sophisticated and modern feel, offering excellent contrast for product details in an e-commerce context.
- Primary color: Rich Medium Purple (#9757EA). This hue was chosen to convey innovation and premium quality without relying on traditional corporate blues, standing out well against a darker background.
- Background color: Desaturated Dark Purple (#1A161C). This dark background is visibly derived from the primary color's hue, ensuring aesthetic consistency while providing a subtle depth.
- Accent color: Light Periwinkle Blue (#C4D0E6). This analogous color provides a softer, brighter contrast to the primary purple, ideal for secondary elements and highlights, making them distinct yet harmonious.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif. Its modern, machined, and neutral aesthetic ensures high readability and professionalism, suitable for both extensive text blocks and concise product information.
- Use clear, intuitive, and vector-based icons. Focus on universally recognizable glyphs for actions like adding to cart, searching, navigating accounts, and indicating order status. Maintain a consistent style (e.g., outlined or filled) across the entire platform for coherence.
- Implement a modular and responsive layout that gracefully adapts to various screen sizes. Prioritize clear information hierarchy, organized product displays, and accessible navigation. Desktop views should utilize efficient multi-column structures, while mobile should leverage bottom sheets and drawers for filters and menus to optimize usability.
- Integrate subtle, functional, and fast animations for interactive elements such as adding items to the cart, opening modals, or transitions in product galleries. Animations should provide clear user feedback and enhance the perceived speed and responsiveness of the platform without being distracting.
